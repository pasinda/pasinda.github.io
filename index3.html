Software testing and Development

Software quality testers group base on Software testing company (SQA). They are conducting software testing trainings, resume reviewing, group meetings and placements for the students. The company and the trainers work closely with the recruiters and employers in Bay area. According to the group discussion and the company information almost all the students who compete their Software QA bootcamps get placements in bay area companies.

Boot camp

Boot camps durations normally go up to 6 weeks. Group class size between 3-6 members . some of them attend in person and some student communicate online . During the Testing they cover performance testing , load testing, penetration testing, Web security testing etc.. most interesting part of the boot camp is hands on training on live projects. In This 6 weeks boot camp students get training on JAVA, Linux, SQL, Web Driver , etc..
